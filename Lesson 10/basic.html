About Me

My name is Jevon Hodge and I am a designer based in Raleigh, North Carolina. I believe that graphic design has paved the way for me to be more creative, precise, and diligent in my creative process . I enjoy creating different forms of design such as, digital illustration, video editing, simple animations, sneaker customization, and product design. My goal is to pursue a career in user experience / visual design, so I am constantly trying to find different opportunities to sharpen my technical skills as often as possible. I am willing to do whatever it takes to reach my goals and to leave my mark as a passionate, ambitious and influential designer for future generations to come.
